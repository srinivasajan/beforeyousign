# Product Roadmap

## Vision

Democratize legal comprehension by making contract analysis accessible, affordable, and actionable for everyone — leveling the playing field between individuals and corporations in contractual negotiations.

## Competitive Advantages

1. **AI-First**: Purpose-built prompts for contract analysis
2. **User-Centric**: Designed for non-lawyers, not legal professionals
3. **Actionable**: Not just analysis, but recommendations
4. **Transparent**: Clear pricing, no hidden costs
5. **Community**: Learn from crowd-sourced contract wisdom

---

## Risk Mitigation

### Product Risks
- **AI accuracy**: Continuous validation, human review option
- **Legal liability**: Clear disclaimers, insurance, legal counsel partnerships
- **User trust**: Transparent methodology, case studies, testimonials

### Business Risks
- **Competition**: First-mover advantage, rapid iteration
- **Regulation**: Monitor legal AI regulations, adapt quickly
- **Market adoption**: Focus on pain points, aggressive marketing

### Technical Risks
- **AI costs**: Optimize prompts, cache results, tiered models
- **Scalability**: Cloud-native architecture, auto-scaling
- **Security**: SOC 2 compliance, encryption, regular audits
